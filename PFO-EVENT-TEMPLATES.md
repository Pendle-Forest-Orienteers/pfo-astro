# PFO event templates

Copy-paste-ready bodies for the three event types PFO runs most often.
Replace anything in `[SQUARE BRACKETS]` with the real values, keep the
rest as-is. All examples follow correct markdown — including the
`| --- | --- |` divider rows that make tables actually render.

---

## How to use

1. In the CMS, **New event** → fill in the frontmatter fields
   (title, date, location, etc.) using the form.
2. Scroll down to **Long-form description**.
3. Switch the editor to **Markdown mode** (toggle top-right of the editor).
4. Paste the template body that matches your event type.
5. Replace `[BRACKETS]` placeholders with real text.
6. Switch back to **Rich text** to preview, or save as draft to see on the live site.

If you already have a similar past event, you can also use the standard
**copy-paste from a previous event** approach — open last year's
equivalent, copy the long-form body, paste into the new entry, edit
the dates and prices.

## Template 1 — Street-O / midweek urban

Use for evening street events using the **score** format (controls
worth 10 / 20 / 30 / 40 / 50 / 60 points), 60- or 75-minute time limits,
solo / pair / trio entries.

```markdown
## Map and terrain

Street-O competition is on local streets, main roads, parks, ginnels, semi-rural areas and footpaths.
You can choose either a 60 minutes score or a 75 minutes score aimed at faster runners.

**MAP SCALE:** The competition map for this event is **1:10,000**, in simplified format.

## Course information

**Start & Finish:** Nearby.

**Timing** (Start/Finish): SI.

Each individual or team will require one Dibber (collect at registration/download if hired), one clue/answer sheet (collect at registration/download) and one course map (issued at the Start). Plus pen/pencil and a watch.
Example maps and clue sheets of previous events will be available to view at the event centre.

**Scoring:** Controls are numbered 10 to 64. The first digit multiplied by 10 is the score value. Thus there are 10 x 10 points controls, 10 x 20, 10 x 30, 10 x 40, 10 x 50 and 5 x 60. Typically, the higher the score value, the greater the distance and/or difficulty.

**Penalties:** Your course time limit is either 75 minutes or 60 minutes depending on which course you entered.
Thereafter, 1 penalty point will be deducted for each 3 seconds you are late, equivalent to 20 points deducted per minute late.

## Registration and start times

Starts from [6:00PM] to [7:00PM] at minute intervals. You can choose your 15 minute start block when you enter the event at SIEntries.

## Directions and parking

**Event centre:** [VENUE NAME], [TOWN] **[POSTCODE]**

**[Note: £40 charge if dibber is lost during hire period.]**

Online entries will **close at midday on race day**. There will be no EOD for this event.
An individual entry can be amended to a Pair or Trio if you want to add a buddy to run with, but this must be done on-line in advance via SIEntries.

**Rules concerning junior entries:**
```

## Template 2 — Snook-O / themed evening event

Use for the snooker-themed format (red+colour pairs followed by colour
sequence) — Barrowford, Blackburn, Alkincoats etc.

```markdown
## Course information

**First part:** Pot any **Red+Colour** pair, then repeat potting a **different Red+any Colour**, up to a maximum of 11 pairs.
Each Red must be potted only once, but Colours may be repeated. You can stop potting Red+Colour pairs at any time, before commencing to parts 2 and 3.

**Second part:** Pot the **zero value** control (control 150), to signal that you are transferring to the final colour sequence. It will be near to the Yellow control.

**Third part:** Finish by potting the snooker colours in sequence **Yellow** (112) **-> Green** (113) **-> Brown** (114) **-> Blue** (115) **-> Pink** (116) **-> Black** (117), then **Finish**. You can break from the final colour sequence at any time and head for the Finish. You must punch the Finish to stop the clock.

**Penalties:** No penalties if you Finish in under **45** mins, thereafter penalties will be deducted at 5 points per minute (1 pt/12 seconds).

**Scoring values:**

| Control | Points | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Reds (101 – 111) | 1 each | Pot once only |
| Zero (150) | 0 | Transition marker |
| Yellow (112) | 2 | Sequence required |
| Green (113) | 3 | Sequence required |
| Brown (114) | 4 | Sequence required |
| Blue (115) | 5 | Sequence required |
| Pink (116) | 6 | Sequence required |
| Black (117) | 7 | Sequence required |

Maximum possible score = 115. Colour controls will be marked with a double circle for easy recognition.

## Registration and start times

**Starts:** From [18:30] to [19:15]
**Course Close:** At [20:30]

## Directions and parking

**Event centre:** [VENUE], [POSTCODE]

W3W: ///**[three.word.address]**

**Parking:** [PARKING DETAILS]

## Entry details

Pre-entry on **[SI Entries](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?event_id=[ID])** only. **Closing date for entries is 12 noon on the day of the event.**

| Entry type | Senior (M/W26+) | Young Adult (M/W21-25) | Junior (M/W20-) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Solo | £7.00 | £5.00 | £3.00 |
| Senior + 1 (Pair) | £9.00 | — | — |
| Senior + 2 (Trio) | £10.00 | — | — |

Dibber hire £1, but £40 if lost! Collect your hired dibber from Download.

If the event needs to be cancelled the [PFO Event Cancellation Policy](/information/event-cancellation-refund-policy) will apply.

## Facilities

[FACILITIES]

## Dog restrictions

## Contacts and officials
```

## Section headings used across all events

For consistency, the long-form body uses these `##` headings in this
order. Skip any that don't apply to your event:

1. `## Map and terrain`
2. `## Course information`
3. `## Registration and start times`
4. `## Directions and parking`
5. `## Entry details`
6. `## Facilities`
7. `## Dog restrictions`
8. `## Contacts and officials`

Every event detail page on the live site styles `##` headings with the
PFO navy + plum tick. Keep the order consistent and the page will read
well on every event.
