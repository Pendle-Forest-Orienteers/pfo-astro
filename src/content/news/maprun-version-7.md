---
title: "MapRun version 7 is now available"
date: 2024-02-03
summary: "The new MapRun is published to Google Play and the App Store. It replaces MapRun6 and brings the app naming back to its roots."
---

MapRun version 7 is now published to the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store under the name **MapRun**.

This brings us full circle. The original app was MapRun, then MapRunF, followed by MapRun6. With this release, the original MapRun in the app stores has been overwritten by MapRun version 7.

MapRun version 7 has all the features of MapRun6 and will ultimately replace MapRun6 as the recommended app, following a period of overlap.

If you've never used MapRun before, it's the easiest way to take part in our Anytime Orienteering courses — there are no physical controls on the ground, and your phone records the GPS trace of your run.
