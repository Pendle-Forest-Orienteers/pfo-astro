The NWUL was re-started last year (2017) after lapsing for a few years.

Read on for last years results, details of 2018 events and how to nominate areas for the 2019 series.

North West Urban League : 2017 – 2018 – 2019

### 2017

After last being held in 2012, the North West Urban League was re-established in 2017. There were five scoring events:

17th June SELOC NW Sprint champs Skelmersdale

18th June SELOC UKUL Horwich Festival Bolton

15th July MDOC Northern Rocket sprints Birchwood

20th August DEE UKUL Frodsham

15th October MDOC Finland 100 urban Stockport

There were 373 participants across the age categories during the season and full league tables can be found at:
[http://www.oxfordfusion.com/ukul/index.cfm?Year=2017&Org=4&Test=0](http://www.oxfordfusion.com/ukul/index.cfm?Year=2017&Org=4&Test=0)

### 2018

The prospective events to be included in 2018 are shown below and are subject to confirmation from each organising club. Applications to include additional events would also be welcome from any club in the region until end of February.

Please now review your club’s entries, if any, and confirm or otherwise that you wish these to be included in the League and that results can be provided according to the urban age categories. Feel free to provide further description. If in doubt, just ask!

Upon confirming, please also ask organisers to include this link to the league tables in your event publicity, where confirmed events will shortly be listed:

http://www.oxfordfusion.com/ukul/index.cfm?&Org=4
