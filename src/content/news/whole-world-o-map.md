Preview O-terrain anywhere in the world before you travel.

In collaboration with OCAD and with the help of various open map data sources, the young Swiss Andrin Benz has created an automatically generated orienteering map of the whole world.

The map can be accessed from any internet-enabled device. The handling and the basic functions are similar to all common map portals.

You'll find the world's largest orienteering map here: [o-map.ch](https://o-map.ch/)

The maps can be presented in ISOM Standard or ISSOM Sprint format. The example map is of a town centre near you in ISSOM format. Recognise it?
