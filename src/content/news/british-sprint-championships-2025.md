What a weekend. Four PFO members took to the university campus grounds in East Anglia to tackle the British Sprint Championships. It was true multi-level mayhem: fast decisions, even faster feet, and plenty of navigating twists to keep everyone on their toes.

A huge well done to all our athletes who made the long journey from East Lancashire to compete and do the club proud: Jack, Judith, Mark, and of course Emma, who stormed her way to 2nd place in the W35 category.

*PFO & Wendy Carlyle*
