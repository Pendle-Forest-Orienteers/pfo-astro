Six PFO ladies and Richard travelled to Northern Ireland for the Irish Orienteering Championships, enjoying the sunshine, scenery and, of course, the orienteering.

Thursday evening started with an urban race around Ballymena, swiftly followed by the Irish Sprint Champs on Friday evening, where Emma won W35, Kay won W40 and Judith took 2nd in W60.

In between the events there were plenty of adventures, including visits to the Giant's Causeway, beaches, and marking the Rawtenstall Street-O results from afar.
