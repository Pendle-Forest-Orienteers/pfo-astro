Here are some of the Trophies that PFO as a club, or individual PFO members can win

## Awards for the Club
PFO were delighted to be presented with the 2021 BOF Club of the Year award at this years JK in South Wales

## Awards for Club Members

### The 'Broom'.
This is an old PFO Trophy that was presented back to the club after many years away.

It is presented annually by the Chairman to someone who has made outstanding contributions throughout the year.

Current holder - Helen Ellis

### Club Champion Trophy
(aka 'The Liver'). This is awarded to the winner of each years club championship score event.

This is a carving of the iconic Pendle Hill

Current Holder - Chris Goddard
