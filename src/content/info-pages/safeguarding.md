Pendle Forest Orienteers have adopted British Orienteering's Safeguarding and Safety policy. Full details of British Orienteering's Safeguarding Policies can be [viewed here](https://www.britishorienteering.org.uk/safeguardingandsafety).

PFO are committed to ensuring a safe environment for all to enjoy orienteering in.

## Safeguarding

PFO adopts and follows the policies set out by British Orienteering.

- The Child Safeguarding policy document can be found [here](http://bof2.sharepoint.com/:b:/g/Business-Support/EbR4OXR0Ga5JttqFHitzHksBjPxr44ptc0eaEsjMSj-UEA?e=q4ZqHQ).
- The Vulnerable Adult Safeguarding policy can be found [here](https://www.britishorienteering.org.uk/doc/policies-and-guidance/safeguarding/adult-safeguarding-policy).

## Reporting a concern

Should you need to report a Safeguarding incident or concern, there are a couple of options.

Contact the Club Welfare Officer, Kay Hawke, or any member of the club committee. Contact details can be found on the [club contacts page](/contacts).

If you don't feel you can do this, follow [this link to the British Orienteering website](https://www.britishorienteering.org.uk/safeguardreporting) for further details.
