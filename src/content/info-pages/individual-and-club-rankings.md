There are a number of leagues and ranking tables where you can see how well you have done compared to other orienteers in your age class.

There are a series of links below to the main ones.

## UK Orienteering League
[UK Orienteering League](https://www.ukorienteeringleague.org.uk/)

This shows how each club performed at UKOL events last year and the club members that contributed.

PFO finished in 46th position in 2025 and are currently 54th for 2026.

## UK Urban League
[UK Urban League](http://www.oxfordfusion.com/ukul/)

Similar to above, but just for urban events

## North West Urban League[](http://www.oxfordfusion.com/ukul/index.cfm?&Org=4)
[North West Urban League](http://www.oxfordfusion.com/ukul/index.cfm?&Org=4)

Prizes are awarded to winners in each age class (as long as they are members of a NWOA club)

## Yorkshire Urban League
[Yorkshire Urban League](http://www.oxfordfusion.com/ukul/index.cfm?&Org=6)

Like the NW league, this covers events that are organised by Yorkshire clubs and is a well established and supported league.

Lots of these events are fairly local to our area and tend to turn into PFO socials in a café near the event centre.

Prizes for winners of each age class from YHOA clubs, although PFO members regularly knock YHOA members off the top slots !

## Individual Rankings
Individual Rankings can also be viewed by logging into the members area on the BOF web-site and then going to the home page.

In the 'About' section it will show your current ranking points, click on that to then filter to see more detail.

## Yorkshire Super League
Details are not yet available for the 2020 super league but like the Yorkshire Urban League a lot of these events are relatively local to PFO.
