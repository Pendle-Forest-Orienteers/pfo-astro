Pendle Forest Orienteers (PFO) is a constituted, unincorporated members' club. The full constitution document — adopted at the AGM and amended only at subsequent AGMs — sets out our objectives, membership rules, committee structure, finances, and dissolution arrangements.

The current full constitution is available as a PDF download. It will be added here once the new website is live.

## Summary of key points

- PFO exists to promote and develop the sport of orienteering in East Lancashire
- All members may stand for and vote at the Annual General Meeting
- The committee is elected annually and consists of Chair, Secretary, Treasurer and other officers as the meeting agrees
- Annual subscriptions are set at the AGM and reviewed each year
- The club is affiliated to British Orienteering and the North West Orienteering Association

For a copy of the full constitution document or any specific governance question, contact the [Secretary](/contacts), Andy Ellis.
