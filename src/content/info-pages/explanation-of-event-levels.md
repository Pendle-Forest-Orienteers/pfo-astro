There are a variety of levels of events for orienteers to take part in.

- **Training** events are non-competitive, used for training and coaching. Aimed at members of the club but they often attract people from neighbouring clubs, and are ideal for newcomers to try the sport.
- **Local** events are usually low-key and in the localised area. Ideal for newcomers. Most clubs run a mid-week mini series throughout the year.
- **Regional** events attract participants from across the local region.
- **National** events are high-quality competitions that attract people from much further afield.
- **Major** events are the British Championships, the annual JK competition and equivalents.

Details of events from neighbouring clubs can be found on the [Events](/events) page. Details of all events nationally can be found on the [British Orienteering website](https://www.britishorienteering.org.uk/event).
