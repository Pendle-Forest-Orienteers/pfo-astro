The table below shows the orienteering age class for a given age of competitor.

*This differs from the classifications used for fell running and other sports.*

When entering orienteering events, the age class below is prefixed by **M** for Men or **W** for Women.

| Your age in current year* | Orienteering age class |
| --- | --- |
| **Junior age classes (M / W)** | |
| 10 and under | M/W 10 |
| 12 and under | M/W 12 |
| 14 and under | M/W 14 |
| 16 and under | M/W 16 |
| 18 and under | M/W 18 |
| 20 and under | M/W 20 |
| **Adult age classes (M / W)** | |
| Any age | M/W 21 |
| 35 and over | M/W 35 |
| 40 and over | M/W 40 |
| 45 and over | M/W 45 |
| 50 and over | M/W 50 |
| 55 and over | M/W 55 |
| 60 and over | M/W 60 |
| 65 and over | M/W 65 |
| 70 and over | M/W 70 |
| 75 and over | M/W 75 |
| 80 and over | M/W 80 |

*Your age should be the age you will be at the **end** of the current year.

**Examples**

- A girl who is 13 in February has the same age class as someone who has their 14th birthday in December — both are **W14**.
- A man born in March 1960 has the same age class as one born in November 1956 — both are **M60**.
