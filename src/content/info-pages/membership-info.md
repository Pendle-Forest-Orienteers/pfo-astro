How to join and the benefits of membership of Pendle Forest Orienteers.

If you plan to orienteer regularly you should join Pendle Forest Orienteers, the North West Orienteering Association and the British Orienteering Federation. When joining PFO you automatically become a member of both NWOA and BOF. These bodies are responsible for the management and development of the sport and need the financial support of participants. Most funding (government & public) is based on the sports membership numbers and participation, so it's important that you are officially recorded as an orienteer.

Read more about the benefits of membership on the [British Orienteering website.](https://www.britishorienteering.org.uk/join)
