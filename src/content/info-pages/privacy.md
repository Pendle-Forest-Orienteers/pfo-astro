This privacy statement explains how Pendle Forest Orienteers (PFO) collects, uses and looks after the personal information you provide when you take part in our events, become a member, or contact us.

## What data we collect

When you enter an event with PFO — through SI Entries, on the day, or as a member — we may collect:

- Your name
- Your email address and phone number
- Your British Orienteering membership number and age class
- Emergency contact details (for forest events)
- Your performance data: course chosen, finishing time, splits

When you become a club member, British Orienteering also holds your full membership record. PFO can see and update your record but does not separately store it.

## Why we collect it

- To organise events safely (knowing who's on a course, knowing who hasn't returned)
- To publish results and post-event reports
- To comply with British Orienteering's rules and insurance requirements
- To send you the club newsletter and time-sensitive event updates (members only)
- To respond to questions you send via the contact form

## What we publish

Results pages on this website show your name, course, time and splits. By participating in our events you accept that this information is published. If you would prefer your name not to appear, contact the event organiser before the start.

Photographs taken at our events may appear on this website, our social media channels, and in newspaper reports. If you'd prefer not to be photographed, please tell the event organiser at registration.

## Your data rights

You have the right to:

- Ask what data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Ask us to delete data we no longer need
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact the [Membership Secretary](/contacts) (Emma Taylor) or the [Secretary](/contacts) (Andy Ellis).

## Cookies

This website uses no tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party fingerprinting. The British Orienteering news widget on the homepage loads from britishorienteering.org.uk, which is a separate organisation with its own privacy policy.

## Changes to this statement

This page is reviewed annually by the committee and updated as required. The British Orienteering data we rely on is governed by their privacy policy, available on their website.
