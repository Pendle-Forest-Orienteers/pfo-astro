In addition to the MaprunF and Permanent Orienteering courses, there are also a small number of courses available to run anytime.

**Simple paper based courses**.

- I**t is important that when running any of these courses you stick to Public Rights of Way and ensure gates are closed after you go through.**- **There is no requirement to climb walls or fences when taking part.**
1. [Bathtime!](/documents/virtual-maps/Bathtime-v2a.pdf) Short OS map based course to north of Marl Pits. 18th May -The feature at CTRL 6 has been removed, but the location should be obvious.

2. [Bromley Cross One](/documents/virtual-maps/Bromley-Cross-One-v2.pdf). OS based course around Jumbles Res

3. [Hapton One](/documents/virtual-maps/Hapton-One.pdf). OS based course around Hapton

4. [Hapton Two](/documents/virtual-maps/Hapton-Two.pdf). OS based course around Hapton

5. [Hapton Three](/documents/virtual-maps/Hapton-Three.pdf). OS based course around Hapton

6. [Hapton Four](/documents/virtual-maps/Hapton-Four.pdf). OS based course around Hapton

7. [Hapton Five](/documents/virtual-maps/Hapton-Five.pdf). OS based course around Hapton. Clue sheet for control locations.

8. [Egerton One](/documents/virtual-maps/Egerton-One-v2.pdf). OS based course to East of Delph Res

**'No Results' Routegadget events**

These events will be set up in [Routegadget](https://www.pfo.routegadget.co.uk/RG2/)
You can select an Event, upload a GPS file, manually enter your time to complete and therefore share your results with others.
You will also be able to print off a paper map of the course, details will be in the table below.

The following 'no results' Routegadget events have been set up:

- [Lockdown Cloughfold 1](https://www.pfo.routegadget.co.uk/rg2/#138)- [Lockdown Maprun Rstall Two](https://www.pfo.routegadget.co.uk/rg2/#139)
