PFO maintains seven Permanent Orienteering Courses (POCs) across East Lancashire. Each course uses fixed posts in a local park or wood with orienteering kite markers attached — pick up the map, try it at your own pace, and tick off the controls.

Most courses come at a few difficulty levels (Easy / Yellow through to Hard). Maps are usually free to download below; some can also be picked up in person from a local point listed on each course.
