## Map and terrain

Newly drawn map, by Alastair Pedley EPOC, at 1:4000, sprint scale.
Corporation Park is well featured sloping parkland, with plenty of vegetation, water, landform, rock and other unusual features in addition to the usual park features.

## Directions and parking

Parking will be roadside on West Park Road, Blackburn. There is free parking (weekends only) on one side, or the other, of this road. Overflow parking will be on nearby side streets.
Approximate location BB2 6DG.
There is a pedestrian entrance to Corporation Park from West Park Road at W3W ///**melt.cotton.dust**
This leads to the cafe where you will find dibber collection, download and nearby Starts and Finish.
Use only this on-foot entrance. Look for PFO flag.

## Entry details

**Registration:** ALL entries must be made through [SI-Entries](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?event_id=13045).
If you are entering as a pair (or small group), it is only necessary to register one lead name, pay for one entry and hire one dibber.

Entries will close on Saturday 2nd March at 23:59, but entries made on or after 1st March will be subject to map availability and will be at a higher price.
