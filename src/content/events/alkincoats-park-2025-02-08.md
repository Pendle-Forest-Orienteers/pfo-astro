## Map and terrain

Fast runnable parkland but with areas of rough ground and scattered trees, plus substantial areas of woodland. Avoid running on games pitches if a game is in progress.

## Facilities

We have sole use of the event centre which has toilets, a changing area, a kitchen and parking. Overspill parking will be on Birtwistle Avenue or side streets.

The Pavillion Cafe, BB8 9QQ, by the bowling greens, serves excellent home cooked meals and snacks every day of the week. About 700m from the event centre.

Nearby shopping opportunities in Colne at Sainsbury's and Boundary Mill, which also has cafe facilities.

## Dog restrictions

Dogs are welcome in Alkincoats Park. However we recommend they do not accompany you on your course. After your run, they may be exercised freely under control. Doggy etiquette applies.
