A 45-minute urban score event on a brand-new map. Mass start at 7:00pm with course close at 8:00pm.

## Course information

One course with twenty controls, ten points each. Maximum score 200 points. Time limit 45 minutes. Penalties for late finishers: one point per three seconds (twenty points per minute). Time and points-based scoring with full SI timing and instant results at the finish.

## Map and terrain

Typical interlinked urban housing estates with many paths, cut-throughs and route choice. Mapped in full urban detail to give newcomers plenty of features to navigate by, encouraging exploration at your own pace.

## Parking

On-street parking in surrounding streets, including Padiham Road where the Social Club is located.

## Safety

Give way to members of the public and other competitors throughout your visit. There are numerous road crossings on the course — take care at all times. Run wide around corners. Wear hi-viz so that you can be seen. Move away from control sites as quickly as possible.
