Hurstwood sits above Hurstwood Reservoir, just east of Burnley. It's classic Lancashire moorland — open hill with rough grass and heather, a mosaic of plantation woodland, and the remains of older industrial workings dotted across the higher ground.

A frequent venue for PFO regionals because the terrain rewards real route choice and physical orienteering. Used for the British Orienteering 2018 National event.

## Access and parking

Park at Hurstwood Reservoir (small free car park) or, for larger events, at Mereclough using event-day signed parking.

## Hazards

Open moorland — weather can change quickly. In wet conditions some streams are uncrossable. Watch for old mine workings on the higher ground.
