Brun Valley Forest Park is PFO's headline venue and the home of the annual Regional fixture. Mixed forest, open meadow, the river Brun winding through the middle, and a network of paths and tracks.

Used for the May 2026 Regional and Yvette Baker Trophy round.

## Access

Park at Heasandford on the eastern side or at Brun Lea on the west, depending on event setup.
